Recent surveys indicate that only a small minority of UK residents support increasing fossil fuel production, including coal, to enhance energy security. For instance, a YouGov poll found that just 8% of Britons believe increasing fossil fuel production is the best way to improve energy security, while 56% prefer more renewables.
