**Project Overview:**
- Samsø, an island in Denmark, became a world-renowned case study for renewable energy transition, achieving energy self-sufficiency based on renewable energy within a decade.

**Community Involvement:**
- The transition was driven by local engagement, with residents actively participating in project development and investment.

**Financing:**
