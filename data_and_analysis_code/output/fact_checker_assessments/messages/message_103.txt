==== CLAIM 5 ====

** Our veracity score (0 = FALSE, 100 = TRUE) **
100/100

** Our explanation for veracity score **
Sustainability-Linked Bonds (SLBs) are financial instruments where the bond's terms, such as interest rates, are linked to the issuer's achievement of predefined sustainability goals. This structure aligns financial returns with environmental performance, incentivizing issuers to meet their sustainability targets. Unlike green bonds, which require proceeds to be used for specific environmental projects, SLBs allow funds to be used for general purposes, provided the issuer meets the agreed-upon sustainability performance targets. This flexibility makes SLBs accessible to a broader range of issuers and encourages comprehensive sustainability commitments. ([napglobalnetwork.org](https://napglobalnetwork.org/innovative-financing/sustainability-linked-bonds/))
