Therefore, the claim is accurate.

** Our veracity score (0 = FALSE, 100 = TRUE) **
100/100

** Our explanation for veracity score **
The claim is accurate. The UK government announced in 2020 its intention to phase out coal-fired power stations by October 2024, a year earlier than the initial 2025 target.
